About Pick My Place

How It Works

A personalized travel recommendation experience built for fast inspiration.

What Pick My Place Does

Pick My Place helps you find travel destinations that match your interests, budget, climate preference, travel mood, and preferred regions.

The destination catalog currently includes 33 curated places across several regions. Ranked results and the Surprise me button both draw from that same catalog.

How Recommendations Are Chosen

The app ranks destinations by scoring how closely your preferences align with each location profile, then shows the best-ranked matches with personalized reasons.

Saved picks

On the results page, each destination has Save this pick (it toggles to Saved ✓ when active). Saved places appear under Your saved picks—a shortlist stored in your browser so it can survive a refresh or a new quiz run.

Clear Saved Results clears only your latest ranked list and preference summary; your saved picks stay. Clear Saved Picks empties the shortlist entirely. On each saved card, Remove from saved drops just that destination.

Surprise me

On the home page, Surprise me — random place skips the matcher and draws one destination at random from the same curated catalog used for rankings. Your form answers are not used for that spin—the results page switches to serendipity mode with copy written for that wild card.

Use it whenever you want a fresh idea without filling the form; go back home and tap again for another random place.
